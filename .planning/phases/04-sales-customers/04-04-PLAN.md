<objective>
Deliver the customer track: full CRUD at `/customers` (D-06/CST-01) with Cyrillic-safe instant search, plus the customer detail page with purchase history (CST-02). The service maintains a `search_lc` lowercase shadow in Python (SQLite cannot fold Cyrillic) and reads the FROZEN `unit_price_cents` for history — never the current card price. This track is independent of the sale-form picker (04-05); it only shares nav + router wiring with the sale slice.

Purpose: customers are the entity a sale optionally links to (SAL-03) and the surface where the operator sees "what/when/at what price" per customer.
Output: customers service, customers routes, five customer templates + nav wiring.
</objective>
