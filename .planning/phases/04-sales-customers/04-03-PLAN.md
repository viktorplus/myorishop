<objective>
Refine the sale slice with the oversell safety flow (SAL-04). Inside `register_sale`, after per-line validation but BEFORE any write, aggregate requested quantity per `product_id` across all basket lines (Pitfall 6) and compare to the cached `Product.quantity`. If any product oversells and `confirm != "1"`, return an oversell signal that the route renders as `partials/sale_oversell.html` with ZERO writes (D-08). The «Продать всё равно» button re-POSTs the same basket via HTMX `hx-vals='{"confirm": "1"}'`; on confirm the check is skipped, the sale writes, and stock may go negative (D-09). «Вернуться к корзине» dismisses the warning.

Purpose: prevents accidental silent oversells while honoring the ledger-is-truth, allow-negative decision. This is an error-state refinement of the 04-02 basket slice.
Output: the oversell branch in register_sale, the oversell partial, and the route/form wiring.
</objective>
