<objective>
Deliver the storage and capture half of PRICE-01: an optional, nullable `min_sale_cents` column on `Product` (D-06 — no global-settings fallback, unlike `low_stock_threshold`/`stale_days`), a new product-form field to set/clear/zero it, and audit-trail coverage identical to the existing cost/sale/catalog price fields. This plan does NOT touch the sale-time guardrail check (that is plan 07-03, which depends on the schema this plan creates).

Purpose: PRICE-01's sale-time warning (07-03) needs `Product.min_sale_cents` to exist and be settable before it can compare an entered sale price against it. Rather than a schema-only plan, this delivers the full observable capability: the operator opens a product card, sets (or clears, or explicitly zeroes) the minimum price, and sees it persist and show up in the price/edit audit trail like `cost_cents`/`sale_cents`/`catalog_cents`.
Output: migration 0006, `Product.min_sale_cents`, `create_product`/`update_product` extended to parse and persist it (joined into `_PRICE_FIELDS` for audit purposes per RESEARCH.md Open Question #1), one new product-form field, and a `price_history.html` label for it.
</objective>
