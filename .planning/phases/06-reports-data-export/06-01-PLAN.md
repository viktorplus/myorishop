<objective>
Give the operator a way to configure, per product, the two threshold values that Phase 6's stock and top/stale reports will read: "low stock" (RPT-02) and "days without a sale = stale" (RPT-04). This is D-04 (per-product, configurable on the product card) plus the D-05 global-fallback settings the later report plans depend on.

Purpose: RPT-02 (low-stock list) and RPT-04 (stale-products list) both need an "effective threshold" per product (per-product value if set, else a global default) to exist before they can be built. Rather than a schema-only plan, this delivers the full observable capability: the operator opens a product card, sets (or clears) these two numbers, and sees them persist and show up in the price/edit audit trail like any other field.
Output: migration 0005, `Product.low_stock_threshold`/`Product.stale_days`, `settings.low_stock_threshold`/`settings.stale_days`, two new product-form fields wired end-to-end through create/update.
</objective>
