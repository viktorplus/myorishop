<objective>
Lay the append-only cash-ledger foundation: a `cash_movements` table that mirrors
`Operation`'s sync-ready shape (UUID PK, signed `amount_cents`, `device_id`/`seq`,
`created_at`/`created_by`, nullable `sale_id` FK) but drops all stock-specific
columns, guarded by DB-level BEFORE UPDATE/DELETE triggers installed in BOTH
`app/db.py` (the live source test fixtures loop over) AND migration `0013`.

Purpose: Nothing else in the phase works without this table + its immutability
guarantee. This implements the storage tier of D-00a and D-03.
Output: `CashMovement` class, `CASH_CATEGORIES`, cash triggers in `app/db.py`,
migration `0013_cash_movements.py`, and the append-only test contract.
</objective>
